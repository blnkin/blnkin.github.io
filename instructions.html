HOW TO PLAY

  • The objective of the game is to remember the highlighted boxes and click on them within the given time limit.
  • Press the "START" button to begin the game. Boxes will be briefly highlighted.
  • Once the boxes are no longer highlighted, the timer will start, and you have 10 seconds to click on the boxes you remember being highlighted.
  • If you click on a correct box, it will turn green. If you click on an incorrect box, it will turn red and the game will end.
  • To win the game, successfully click on all the highlighted boxes within the time limit.
  • If you win or lose, the game will return to the home screen, and you can try again by navigating back to the level.
